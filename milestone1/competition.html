Competition

Who is our competition?

B's Lost and Found is a large antique store with several vendors inside. Us being one of them, it is important to keep our booth cooler than everyone elses so our things sell.

What other booths are successfully doing to gain a large profit

Other booths in the store use very beautiful decorating to display their items and it makes their antiques more presentable and purchaseable.

What other booths are not succesfully doing to gain profit

Some of the booths in the store have their items marked up very expensive and it makes their things not sell as easily.

What I can do better than other booths

To make my booth better than others, I can mark my items at a reasonable price and display things pretty.
